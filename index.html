Hello adventurer!

Welcome to the Weakling, an open source RPG game.

This is just the beginning and very much work in progress on it's early stage, but there are already a few things you can do to explore it yourself!

Please, use WASD or Arrow Keys to navigate the world, and Space to move between locations and interact with certain objects (highlighted with the purple frame). Other actions are done with the mouse.

For more information and project-related links please read an Introduction. Thank you and enjoy!
